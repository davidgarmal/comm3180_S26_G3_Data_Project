## Group project for COMM3180 _Stories from data_ (Spring 2026)

# Overview

In this project, we aim to investigate some questions about the influence of diversity on higher education on various levels. We will use the following datasets:
1. The [College Scorecard](https://collegescorecard.ed.gov/) data from the U.S. Department of Education.
2. The U.S. [Census Data](https://data.census.gov/table).
3. Athletic scholarships and student athlete data.

From this project, we want to understand how the demographic characteristics of schools affect student's earning potential after they graduate. Along the same lines, we want to answer is whether colleges located in particular areas are more likely to have diverse student bodies.
